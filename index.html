Profile

Lead with a short introduction that sounds like a person, not a firm.

A recent graduate does not need polished agency language. She needs a clear summary that explains what she studied, the kinds of work she has already done, and the kind of team she wants to join next.

This section works best as a three- or four-sentence bio covering school, concentration, internships, campus leadership, creative interests, and the full-time roles she is pursuing.
