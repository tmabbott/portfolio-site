



Creative Brief: Eastie Farm x Studio 5 Student Driven Marketing Agency
	•	 Background / Context
From late May through mid-November, Eastie Farm brings local produce to the East Boston neighborhood, selected from what’s in season and ready to harvest from the local partnership farms throughout the growing season. The farm is looking for long and short-form video content to engage with their clientele of English and Spanish speakers.
	•	Business / Communication Challenge
Studio 5 aims to bridge the gap between prospective CSA users and the farm itself, to highlight the amenities in a visually appealing way to encourage people to visit the farm, utilize its resources and donate when possible.
	•	Target Audience
Diverse community members use Eastie Farm’s resources, including people of all races, ethnicities, genders and income levels. Studio 5 wants to reach them through inviting, educational, and easy to comprehend videos describing the workings of a CSA.
	•	Single-Minded Proposition (SMP)
If our target audience remembers one thing, it should be what a CSA is and its benefits.
	•	Support / Reasons to Believe
As a student driven marketing agency, we are motivated to support local businesses in their endeavors to strengthen their communities through communication strategies we are uniquely equipped to implement. Studio 5 has also previously partnered with Eastie Farm, so there is already an established connection between the organizations.
	•	Desired Response
Raising local community awareness about Eastie Farm’s CSA and its greater impact on civic engagement would prove to be a successful campaign.
	•	Mandatories
Included will be both Eastie’s logo, as well as Studio 5’s, at the end of each video. Two long-form videos will be created with the intention of making them clippable for greater usage across social media platforms.
