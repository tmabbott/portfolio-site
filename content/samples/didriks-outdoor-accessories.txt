It’s never too early to start daydreaming about those cozy outdoor gatherings that come along with the warmer months. With Spring just around the corner, now is the perfect time to get a head start on decorating your outdoor space. You’ve just bought from fresh new outdoor furniture (looking for ideas? Check out our recent blog for inspiration), and you want to elevate the space to make it your own, cozy getaway. Here’s our guide to creating the perfect outdoor oasis with gorgeous decor, linens, and lighting.


Outside in the early Spring, comfy throw blankets are your best friend. The Torekov Throw by Skargaarden adds a calm, neutral vibe to any space with its deep blue, sky blue, and gray color palette, while providing adequate warmth with its heavy weave. Cozy up with this peaceful throw while propping your feet up on Cane-Line’s Divine Footstool in Turquoise—a versatile addition to any seating area (indoor or outdoor) which is hand crocheted for extra comfort. If you’re looking for more of a statement from your throw—try Libeco Home’s Redwood Stripe Throw or Montana Throw in Gold. Featuring bold, bright colors of red and gold, and made of 100% Belgian linen and soft wool—what’s not to love?

Want more accessories to cozy up your sitting areas? Check out Chilewich’s Bold Stripe Shag Mat in Punch—a statement rug elevating not only design but also functionality. Being weather-resistant, quick-drying, and good both indoors and outdoors, this versatile floormat is excellent for year-round use. Counter the bright magenta rug with a gentle, deep blue throw pillow like Skargaarden’s Hemse Pillow in Marine Blue to balance the space. For a finishing touch to this look, try Skargarren’s Marstrand Candle Lantern in Light Leather and Glass. An elegant and classic way to provide candlelight after dark, the Marstrand Candle Lantern fits most candle pillars and features a leather handle to conveniently move and protect it from bad weather.

If you’re looking to play up the natural elements of outdoor accessories, consider these Eva Solo Bird Shelters and Hanging Bird Feeders. Bird-watchers and lovers—whether amateurs or professionals—can all enjoy attracting a variety of garden birds into their space. The Bird Shelter is elegant to complement outdoor decorations and also features a woven rush where birds can feel right at home. The Hanging Bird Feeder can be hung on trees or posts and is dishwasher safe and easy to refill. To tend to your garden in an efficient, modern way, consider Iittala’s Set of 2 Watering Bottles. The perfect tool for plant lovers, these watering bottles keep the soil damp so you can relax and enjoy your outdoor haven. Want to light up your garden while maintaining energy efficiency? Check out the SunLight Lamp with Garden Spike from Eva Solo. This lamp is solar-powered and can be placed anywhere along your garden path, thanks to its spike at the bottom. Nature-lovers rejoice!
