






Creative Brief:
Simmons University Admissions x Studio 5 Student Driven Marketing Agency
	•	 Background / Context
Each year, excited students apply to several colleges in hope of finding their unique path through higher education. Simmons University's Office of Admissions
	•	 Business / Communication Challenge
Studio 5 aims to bridge the gap between soon to be college students and Simmons University as an institution. We plan to highlight the amenities in a visually appealing way to encourage people to attend, utilize its resources and claim their spot at Simmons.
	•	Target Audience
Studio 5 set out to create materials for an acceptance packet that would grab the attention of both prospective students, as well as their parents.
	•	Single-Minded Proposition (SMP)
If our audience remembers one thing, it should be what Simmons University has to uniquely offer.
	•	Support / Reasons to Believe
As a student driven marketing agency, we are familiar with the aspects of applying for colleges and the decision making process.
	•	Desired Response
The goal for the acceptance package redesign is to excite prospective students and encourage them to attend SImmons University.
	•	Mandatories
Included will be a redesign of the Simmons acceptance packet pages, bubble mailer, and stickers for new students. There will be an incorporation of some previous elements along with a selection of new, custom art designed by the Studio 5 creative team members.
